About the Artist

Confident.

Jessi Lilli is a rising indie-pop artist whose music blends energetic beats with raw emotional depth. Known for her authentic storytelling and vibrant stage presence, she connects with listeners through songs that feel both personal and universal.

Her sound is bold, her spirit is free — and every melody she writes carries a little piece of her heart. With her debut single "Piano in the Sky", Jessi invites you into a world of longing, hope, and the courage to love.
